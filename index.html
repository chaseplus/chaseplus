Company Overview

With bespoke Interior Design and Fit-out solutions, Chase Interiors provides services across residential and commercial properties by creating new concepts for interiors and exteriors that are well planned and well designed.

At Chase Interiors, the importance of quality for long-term comfort and convenience is recognized and prioritized. With experience across industries – Residential, Corporate, Retail & F&B – our services are specialized to deliver complete design, fit-out and refurbishment of units with supply and installation of furniture.

Our keen focus on customer satisfaction allows us to take a partnership approach when dealing with clients. Trendy interiors, realistic budgets and timelines with attention to detail motivate us to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Our well-rounded and experienced team of professionals delivers quality based on a holistic design formula that ensures results from the moment we step in.
