Design

With a practical design philosophy, we actualize ideas that translate into functional and beautiful spaces. Our in-house team of designers, CAD Technicians and 3D Visualizers are trained to work closely with clients and internal teams to identify design solutions that match our clients’ aesthetics and budgets.

Each design project begins with the initial Concept Phase. During this phase we identify the exact needs of the client, the budget and the envisioned outcome.

Once we visualize each concept through mood boards, sketches and 3D renderings, every detail of the design is presented to the client.

Our goal for every project is to deliver efficient, long-lasting design solutions that are sustainable and flexible to new design trends, and that are pocket-friendly.

When recreating and redesigning spaces, it is important to take time to understand the purpose of the fit-out or refurbishment project.

More comfortable spaces? A themed design aesthetic? Or simply an updated look to outdated interiors?

The goal of any fit-out determines the plan of action for our Fit-out team. However, a constant across every project – every space we reimagine is creative and practical. A functional distribution of spaces that makes each space organized and clutter-free – whether at home, at work or at in a luxury hotel.

To make this process easy for our clients, we assign a Project Manager who ensures a successful project from conception to completion – in the given time, and budget.
